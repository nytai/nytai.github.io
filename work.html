I/She

I walked into a room. All the screens were black, all the whites light.
She wore a denim jacket, stained yellow around the sleeves from cigarette smoke.
She wore a black shirt; blond hair down to her neck.
She wore a striped shirt, sleeves loose, freckled shoulder exposed.
She wore a thin cashmere sweater, translucent. Skin smooth.
She wore a thin chain, silver locket, her neck, tangled around.
She wore translucent smooth, skin cashmere.
She wore, a shoulder exposed, freckled shirt.
She, wore a hair, blond to the neck, black shirt around.
She, wore, a, smoke, stained, yellow, the, sleeves.
They room toward light screens, into I walk.


Glancing

The music played a catchy rhythm, can't remember what.
I sat across the room, ruminating.

Sometimes they look over, some times our eyes meet.

Should I move? Swing forward.
Stare |for a second...| then close my eyes.

Or stay seated?
Swaying my head and wrists to music.

Kindling, the gentle pulse, in chest.
